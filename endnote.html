Congratulations on reducing your body’s acute stress response!

You have most likely offset the activation of the Hypothalamic-Pituitary-Adrenal axis. The HPA axis is your body’s longer-term stress system, activated when stress persists. It releases cortisol, which helps you cope short-term but can strain your body if it stays elevated. Ongoing activation is linked to issues like poor sleep, weakened immunity, and anxiety—so managing stress early can help prevent these longer-term effects.

Keep up the great work!
