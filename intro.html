This happens when your Sympathetic Nervous System is activated—your body’s natural reaction to stress, effort, or stimulants like caffeine. Your brain signals the release of epinephrine and norepinephrine, which make your heart beat faster and stronger to deliver more oxygen to your body. This response helps you stay alert and ready to act, but frequent or unexplained increases may be a sign of ongoing stress.
